About Us

Welcome to The Leaf. We have created this social marketing system as a gaming concept. We register customers and food shops for this system.levels have been created for customers.in the first level we provide five food shops. By buying foods from the shops for more than the amount we give, customers are receiving a code by the shop. The first level is being completed after entering the codes of all five shops into the system. Then the customers are receiving discounts and offers from one of five shops. We do not do any online payment.
